Welcome to my portfolio

Welcome to my photography portfolio! My name is Smeet Patel and I'm a Melbourne-based photographer specializing in Landscape, wildlife and portrait photography. Through my lens, I aim to capture the beauty and emotion of the world around us, whether it's a stunning landscape, a candid moment between two people, or a bustling street scene. I invite you to explore my website and view some of my favorite photos from my portfolio. I hope my work will inspire you and spark your own sense of creativity. If you have any questions or comments, feel free to reach out to me through the contact page. Thank you for visiting and I hope you enjoy my photography!
